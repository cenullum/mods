Crazy Eights - a fast tabletop card game for 2-8 players.

Bold, colorful card faces (big numbers, four colors - purple, teal, orange and
green) with classic Crazy Eights rules: match the top card by color or value,
eights are wild (pick the next color), skip / reverse / +2 keep the table
spicy. First empty hand wins the round.

Sit at the round table and your camera rotates to your seat - the deck and
discard pile are shared in the middle like a real tabletop. Your cards live in
a hand bar at the bottom of YOUR screen only: everyone else (players and
spectators alike) just sees card backs and hand counts, enforced by the host
(server-authoritative, no packet peeking). Join mid-round and you spectate
with everyone's live mouse cursors until the next round starts - one click on
"Sit at table" queues you in.

Built on the engine's card system (the same "cards" JSON the online image
editor exports), so the deck art is easy to reskin.
