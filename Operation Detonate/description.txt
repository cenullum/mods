Operation: Detonate - a push-your-luck sabotage card game for 2-5 agents.

You are a covert ops team pulling equipment from a shared supply deck... in
which one Time Bomb per opponent is hiding. Draw a bomb without a Disarm Kit
and you are out of the mission - the last agent standing wins.

Sabotage tools: Ambush (next agent owes two turns), Retreat (end your turn
without drawing), Supply Request (a chosen agent must hand over a card of
THEIR choice), Mission Shuffle, Recon Drone (peek at the top 3 cards) and the
Signal Jammer - a reactive counter played during the 3.5s "jam window" after
any action; jammers can jam jammers. Collect identical weapons: a pair steals
a random card, a triple lets you name the exact card you want.

Tabletop presentation: sit at the round table and your camera rotates to your
seat. Your hand lives at the bottom of YOUR screen only - everyone else,
including spectators, sees card backs and counts (host-enforced, deck order
never leaves the server). Late joiners spectate with live mouse cursors and
queue in with one click for the next mission.

Card faces are generated from the engine's "cards" JSON (same format the
online image editor exports) with English + Turkish localization built in;
the art PNGs in general/images are flat placeholders - repaint freely.
