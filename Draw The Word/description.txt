Draw The Word - a co-op draw & guess party game.

Players take turns drawing a secret word on a shared board while everyone else
races to guess it in chat. The answer is never spoiled: a correct guess is
announced by name without showing the word, and a near-miss is whispered only
to you.
Guess fast for more points; the drawer earns a bonus for every player who gets
it. Reveal optional letter hints (at the cost of points), report an unfair
drawer to skip their turn, and when everyone has had a turn the top 3 take the
podium. Type /rules in chat for the full guide.

Minimum 2 players. Built on the engine's paintable-canvas system: only the
current drawer can paint the board (server-authoritative), everyone else just
watches.
