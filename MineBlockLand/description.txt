MineBlockLand - a top-down co-op survival island.

Every world is generated from a seed: forests, beaches, rocky hills, farmland,
a shallow sea you can wade through and a deep sea you cannot pass. Chop trees,
mine rock, craft tools (better gear works faster, tires you less and finds
rarer gems), farm melons, and loot the stone dungeon east of the camp.

At night the dead walk. Survive six of them - at nightfall of day 7 the
Guardian of the Isle rises and fills the sky with projectiles. Slay it for the
confetti, the scoreboard and the glory. The world is saved on the host, so you
can come back days later and continue where you left off.

Commands: /friendlyfire (host), /newworld (host), /seed, /stats
