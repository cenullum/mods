Use your grappling hook to swing through challenging levels. Your character has no direct movement - the hook is your only way to navigate the world.

Objective: Reach the golden flag at the top!

Features:
- Grappling hook-based movement system
- Adjustable hook power with progress bar
- Side-scrolling gravity-based gameplay
- Challenging hook and swing mechanics
- Fishing system with different fish types from various locations (sea, lake, cave, oasis, etc.)
- "Finding Liar" mini-game: A social deduction game for 3+ players where everyone knows a word except the liar.
