Football is a multiplayer team-based game where two teams compete to score goals.

- Two teams: Red and Blue
- Hold charge to shot power, release to hit the ball
- Ball physics with bounce and friction
- Scoreboard tracks individual goals and assists
- Match can be played with time limits (1-90 minutes or infinite)
- Players can be moved between teams or made spectators
