Campfire Survivors is a wave-based survival game where you defend against increasingly difficult enemies.

- Waves last 60-120 seconds with 30 second cooldown periods
- Enemies spawn in groups
- 6 enemy types: Ghost, Wolf, Bandit, Zombie, Cactus, and Snake
- Each enemy has unique stats for health, damage, and speed
- Enemies evolve every 7 waves, increasing their health and damage
- Bandit enemies shoot bullets that deal damage based on wave level
- Players can shoot projectiles at enemies
- Game ends when all players are defeated
