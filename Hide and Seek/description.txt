Hide and Seek - a top-down co-op game of disguise and pursuit.

A random cave (rooms, corridors and scattered props) is generated from a seed the
host picks, so everyone plays the exact same map. Players are split into two teams
and swap sides after the first round.

HIDERS paint their own 32x32 body to blend into the props around them. Hit "Paint
Mode" to freeze and open the paint panel (brush 1-32, and the eyedropper can grab
colours straight from the world). Use the Previous/Next/Yourself buttons to look at
other hiders for inspiration. The later you're caught - or if you survive - the more
points you score.

SEEKERS start sealed in a room for a minute while the hiders scatter, then the room
opens and the hunt begins. Left-click to fire a short ray toward your cursor (2s
cooldown, walls block it). The faster you catch a hider, the more points you earn.
Seekers show their Steam avatar, and can repaint their body too if they like.

Score carries across both rounds, then a final table is shown and everyone votes on
the next map. Same seed = same cave every time.
